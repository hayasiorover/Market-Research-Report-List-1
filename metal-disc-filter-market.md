<p><p>The metal disc filter market is characterized by two primary types: wire mesh and laser drilled holes. Wire mesh filters utilize woven strands of metal to create a robust barrier for particle filtration, ideal for high-flow applications. In contrast, laser drilled hole filters involve precision-engineered holes that allow for tailored filtration capabilities and finer filtration. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, offering varying levels of filtration efficiency, durability, and operational benefits across sectors like water treatment and oil refining.</p></p>
