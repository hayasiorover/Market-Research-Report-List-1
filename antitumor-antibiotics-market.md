<p><p>The antitumor antibiotics market includes several key types, each with distinct mechanisms and applications. Etoposide is often used for treating testicular and lung cancers by inhibiting topoisomerase II. Doxorubicin is widely used in various cancers due to its ability to intercalate DNA and disrupt replication. Daunorubicin is primarily employed in treating leukemia, effectively targeting rapidly dividing cells. Bleomycin A5 is used for specific cancers like testicular and Hodgkin's lymphoma, causing DNA strand breakage and reducing tumor growth.</p></p>
