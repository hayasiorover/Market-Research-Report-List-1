<p><p>Mechanical pump seals are essential components that prevent fluid leakage in pumps. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Pusher Seals** utilize a dynamic elastomeric element that allows for slight movement, accommodating wear and maintaining a tight seal over time. </p><p>2. **Non-pusher Seals** have a more rigid design, providing a reliable seal without the need for adjustment, making them suitable for specific applications.</p><p>3. **Cartridge Seals** come as a pre-assembled unit, simplifying installation and maintenance, ideal for various industrial applications.</p></p>
