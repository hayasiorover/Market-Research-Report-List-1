<p><p>The polyester short fiber market is primarily segmented into solid and hollow fibers. Solid fibers are dense, offering strength and durability, making them suitable for applications like clothing and upholstery. In contrast, hollow fibers have a tubular structure, providing better insulation and lightweight properties, ideal for bedding and winter clothing. Both types serve distinct purposes in the textile industry, catering to varying consumer needs for comfort, functionality, and performance, thus influencing market demand and production strategies.</p></p>
