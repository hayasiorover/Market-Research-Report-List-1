<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Field-Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EEPROM</li><li>Antifuse</li><li>SRAM</li><li>Flash</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded FPGA market includes various technologies characterized by their configuration methods. EEPROM-based FPGAs utilize electrically erasable programmable read-only memory for flexibility and reprogramming. Antifuse FPGAs offer high security with a one-time programmable architecture, ideal for applications needing tamper resistance. SRAM-based FPGAs provide rapid reconfiguration and are commonly used in prototyping and high-performance applications. Flash-based FPGAs combine non-volatility with reprogrammability, suitable for low-power designs. Other market types may include specialized architectures catering to niche applications.</p></p>
