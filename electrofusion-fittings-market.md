<p><p>Electrofusion fittings are specialized connectors used in the joining of polyethylene (PE) pipes, primarily in the water and gas distribution industries. The process involves the use of electric heating elements embedded within the fittings, which melt the PE surfaces of the pipes and fittings to create a strong, leak-proof bond.
