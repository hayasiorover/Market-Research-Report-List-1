<p><p>The Aluminum Matrix Composites (AMCs) market is primarily categorized into two types: Particle Enhanced and Fiber Enhanced. Particle Enhanced AMCs incorporate reinforcing particles, such as ceramics, to improve mechanical properties and thermal stability, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, Fiber Enhanced AMCs utilize continuous or short fibers, such as carbon or glass, to provide superior strength and stiffness. Both types offer enhanced performance characteristics, catering to diverse industries seeking lightweight, durable materials.</p></p>
