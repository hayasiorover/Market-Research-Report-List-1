<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) come in various types, primarily categorized as Unit-Load and Mini-Load systems. Unit-Load AS/RS is designed for handling larger pallets or containers, making it ideal for warehouses needing to store bulk items efficiently. In contrast, Mini-Load AS/RS focuses on smaller items, such as boxes or individual products, enhancing order picking and storage efficiency in facilities with high product variety. Both types streamline operations, reduce labor costs, and optimize space utilization.</p></p>
