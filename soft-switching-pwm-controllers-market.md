<p><p>Soft Switching PWM controllers are vital components in power conversion applications, with two primary market types: AC-DC and DC-DC. AC-DC controllers convert alternating current to direct current, optimizing efficiency and reducing electromagnetic interference during the process. Conversely, DC-DC controllers manage the conversion between different DC voltage levels, enabling efficient energy transfer in various electronic devices. Both types leverage soft-switching techniques to minimize switching losses, enhance thermal performance, and improve overall system reliability in power management solutions.</p></p>
