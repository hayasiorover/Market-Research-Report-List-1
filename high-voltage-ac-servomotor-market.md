<p><strong>The High Voltage AC Servomotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Voltage AC Servomotor market is primarily divided into two types: synchronous and asynchronous servomotors. Synchronous servomotors operate at a constant speed, synchronized with the supply frequency, offering precision and efficiency, making them suitable for applications requiring accurate control. In contrast, asynchronous servomotors, also known as induction motors, do not operate in sync with the supply frequency, resulting in variable speeds. They are typically used in applications where torque and robustness are prioritized, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
