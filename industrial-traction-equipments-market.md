<p><p>The industrial traction equipment market comprises three key components: traction transformers, traction motors, and traction converters. Traction transformers step down high voltages for efficient power distribution in electrified rail systems. Traction motors convert electrical energy into mechanical power, propelling trains and enhancing acceleration. Traction converters manage and convert electrical energy between AC and DC formats, optimizing motor performance and energy efficiency. Together, these components are crucial for reliable and efficient operation in rail transport and electrification systems.</p></p>
