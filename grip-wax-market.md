<p><p>The grip wax market for skiing features three main types: hard wax, klister wax, and spray wax. Hard wax is used for cold, dry conditions, providing excellent grip on compact snow. Klister wax is designed for warmer temperatures and wet, sticky snow, offering better adhesion and glide. Spray wax, a convenient option, enhances grip and glide through quick application. Each type caters to different weather and snow conditions, allowing skiers to optimize their performance based on environmental factors.</p></p>
