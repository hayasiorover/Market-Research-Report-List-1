<p><p>The single crystal germanium market is categorized into three main types: Solar Grade, Infrared Grade, and Detector Grade. Solar Grade germanium is primarily used in photovoltaic cells for solar panels, optimizing energy conversion. Infrared Grade germanium is utilized in optical applications, particularly in infrared optics and windows, due to its excellent transparency in the infrared spectrum. Detector Grade germanium is employed in radiation detection and imaging applications, prized for its high purity and sensitivity to gamma rays and X-rays.</p></p>
