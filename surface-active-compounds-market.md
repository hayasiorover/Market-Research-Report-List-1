<p><p>The surface-active compounds market comprises four main types: anionic surfactants, which have a negative charge and are commonly used in cleaners and detergents; non-ionic surfactants, which are neutral and effective in different pH levels, making them ideal for formulations requiring mildness; cationic surfactants, positively charged and used in hair conditioners and fabric softeners; and amphoteric surfactants, which can act as either cationic or anionic depending on the environment, offering versatility in various applications.</p></p>
