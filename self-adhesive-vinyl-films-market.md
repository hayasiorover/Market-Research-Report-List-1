<p><ul><li>Translucent</li><li>Transparent</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-adhesive vinyl films are categorized into three primary types based on their light-blocking properties: translucent, transparent, and opaque. Translucent films allow partial light transmission, making them ideal for privacy while still permitting some visibility. Transparent films permit clear visibility and are commonly used for signage and window applications. Opaque films block all light, providing total privacy and a solid color finish, often utilized for branding, advertising, or decorative purposes on surfaces where visibility is not desired.</p></p>
