<p><p>Loop-Mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) is a nucleic acid amplification technique known for its efficiency and simplicity. Innovations in LAMP technology, including the development of portable devices and integrated systems, are enhancing its accessibility and ease of use. Additionally, the rise of personalized medicine and the push for decentralized healthcare solutions are propelling the adoption of LAMP-based assays.
