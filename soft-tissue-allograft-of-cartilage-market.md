<p><p>The soft tissue allograft cartilage market comprises three main types: hyaline cartilage, elastic cartilage, and fibrocartilage. Hyaline cartilage is the most common, providing smooth surfaces for joint movement and support in respiratory structures. Elastic cartilage offers flexibility and maintains shape, found in structures like the ear. Fibrocartilage, known for its toughness and shock-absorbing properties, is present in intervertebral discs and joint cartilage. Each type serves distinct purposes in tissue repair and reconstruction, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
