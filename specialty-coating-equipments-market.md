<p><p>The specialty coating equipment market includes various technologies such as Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD), and thermal spray equipment. PVD equipment applies thin films through vaporization, enhancing surface properties. CVD equipment, utilizing chemical reactions, provides coatings with improved performance and durability. Thermal spray equipment, on the other hand, deposits molten or semi-molten materials to create protective layers. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, contributing to advancements in coatings and material performance.</p></p>
