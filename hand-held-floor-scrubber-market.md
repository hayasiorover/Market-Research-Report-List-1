<p><p>The hand-held floor scrubber market is divided into two main types: AC Type and DC Type. AC Type scrubbers are powered by alternating current, offering consistent performance for heavy-duty cleaning tasks, typically used in larger commercial settings. In contrast, DC Type scrubbers operate on direct current, providing enhanced portability and flexibility, making them ideal for smaller areas or residential use. Both types cater to various cleaning needs, with AC types focusing on power and durability, while DC types emphasize convenience and maneuverability.</p></p>
