<p><strong>The CMTS/QAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS)</li><li>Modular CMTS (M-CMTS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMTS/QAM market is categorized into two main types: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). I-CMTS brings all functionalities into a single chassis, simplifying deployment and maintenance, making it suitable for smaller operators or less complex networks. In contrast, M-CMTS separates control and data planes, allowing operators to scale networks flexibly and efficiently. This modularity supports high-capacity demands, making M-CMTS ideal for larger, more dynamic environments requiring extensive bandwidth and advanced features.</p></p>
