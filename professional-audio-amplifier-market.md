<p><ul><li>Class A</li><li>Class B</li><li>Class AB</li><li>Class G</li><li>Class H</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional audio amplifier market is segmented into various classes based on their design and efficiency. Class A amplifiers are known for their high sound quality but are less efficient. Class B amplifiers offer better efficiency but can introduce distortion. Class AB amplifiers combine the benefits of Class A and B, striking a balance between sound quality and efficiency. Class G amplifiers enhance efficiency by adjusting the power supply based on demand, while Class H amplifiers utilize a similar concept with dynamic voltage levels for improved performance.</p></p>
