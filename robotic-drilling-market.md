<p><p>The robotic drilling market is divided into two main types: retrofit and new builds. Retrofit involves upgrading existing drilling rigs with robotic technologies to enhance efficiency and reduce labor costs, allowing operators to modernize their equipment without complete replacement. In contrast, new builds refer to the construction of entirely new drilling rigs that integrate advanced robotic systems from the outset, offering superior automation and technological capabilities. Both approaches aim to improve operational performance and safety in drilling operations.</p></p>
