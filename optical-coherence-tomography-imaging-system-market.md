<p><strong>The Optical Coherence Tomography Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-Domain Type</li><li>Spectral-Domain Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) imaging system market is primarily categorized into two types: Time-Domain OCT (TD-OCT) and Spectral-Domain OCT (SD-OCT). TD-OCT utilizes a time-based measurement to capture images, offering lower resolution but simpler design and cost. Conversely, SD-OCT employs spectral analysis for faster imaging with higher resolution capabilities, making it more popular in clinical applications. Both types are crucial in providing detailed, cross-sectional images of biological tissues, particularly in ophthalmology and cardiology.</p></p>
