<p><p>The DP 20 Retimer and Redriver market focuses on two key types of devices that enhance DisplayPort 2.0 signal integrity. A retimer regenerates and resynchronizes digital signals, ensuring high-quality transmission over longer distances, which is crucial for high-resolution displays. In contrast, a redriver amplifies and reshapes signals to maintain signal strength over shorter distances. Both technologies are essential for addressing bandwidth demands of advanced video applications, optimizing performance in consumer electronics, professional displays, and data centers.</p></p>
