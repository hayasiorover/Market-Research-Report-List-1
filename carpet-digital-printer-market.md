<p><p>The carpet digital printer market is primarily divided into two main types: D-series and S-series. The D-series focuses on direct-to-fabric printing, providing high-quality, vibrant designs suitable for diverse carpet materials. In contrast, the S-series emphasizes speed and efficiency, often employing dye-sublimation techniques for quicker production while maintaining satisfactory print quality. Both series cater to different customer needs, with the D-series ideal for intricate designs and short runs, and the S-series suitable for bulk production and rapid turnaround.</p></p>
