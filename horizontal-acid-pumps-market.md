<p><p>The horizontal acid pumps market is primarily categorized into multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, allowing them to handle higher pressure applications, making them suitable for transporting corrosive liquids over long distances. In contrast, single-stage pumps are designed for lower pressure applications and are typically more compact, offering ease of maintenance and efficiency for shorter runs. Both types are essential in various industries, including chemical processing and mining, where handling acids and aggressive fluids is crucial.</p></p>
