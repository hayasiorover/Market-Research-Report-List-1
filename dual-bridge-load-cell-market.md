<p><p>The Dual Bridge Load Cell market comprises three main types: Pressure Type, Strain Type, and Capacitive Type. Pressure Type load cells measure force exerted on a diaphragm, converting pressure into an electrical signal. Strain Type load cells use strain gauges to detect deformation under load, providing precise measurements. Capacitive Type load cells operate on variable capacitance, sensing changes in dielectric properties in response to applied force. Each type serves diverse applications in industrial automation, weighing systems, and material testing.</p></p>
