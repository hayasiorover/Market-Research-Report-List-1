<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide Laser market can be categorized into three main types: continuous output, pulse output, and Q-switch output. Continuous output lasers emit a steady beam, ideal for cutting and engraving materials. Pulse output lasers deliver short bursts of energy, allowing for precise applications like surgical procedures. Q-switch output lasers produce high-energy pulses in rapid succession, enabling advanced applications in tattoo removal and material processing. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, including healthcare and manufacturing.</p></p>
