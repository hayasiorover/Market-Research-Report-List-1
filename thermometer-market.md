<p><ul><li>Liquid-Filled Thermometers</li><li>Digital Thermometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermometer market primarily comprises liquid-filled thermometers and digital thermometers. Liquid-filled thermometers utilize capillary tubes filled with liquids like mercury or alcohol, expanding with temperature changes, providing a visual reading. They are reliable and low-cost but less favored for safety concerns. Digital thermometers, on the other hand, use electronic sensors to provide quick and accurate temperature readings on a digital display. They are user-friendly and can feature additional functionalities, making them increasingly popular for home and medical use.</p></p>
