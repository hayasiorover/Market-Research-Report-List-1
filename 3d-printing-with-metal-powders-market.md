<p><ul><li>DMLS</li><li>SLM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printing market with metal powders primarily includes two key technologies: Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). DMLS utilizes laser energy to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with excellent mechanical properties, suitable for functional prototypes and end-use parts. In contrast, SLM involves fully melting the metal powder, resulting in dense and highly detailed components. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including aerospace and medical, due to their ability to create lightweight yet strong structures.</p></p>
