<p><p>The Coronary Artery Disease medical devices market comprises several key products. Coronary stents are mesh tubes inserted into narrowed arteries to keep them open. PTCA (Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty) balloon catheters are used to widen blocked arteries by inflating a balloon at the site of a blockage. Coronary guidewires are thin wires that facilitate the navigation and placement of other devices within the coronary arteries. Together, these devices play a crucial role in treating coronary artery disease.</p></p>
