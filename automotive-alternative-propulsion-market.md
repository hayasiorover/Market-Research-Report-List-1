FCEVs use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, emitting only water as a byproduct. HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, optimizing fuel efficiency by switching between power sources. EVs operate solely on electricity stored in batteries, producing zero tailpipe emissions. Each type aims to reduce environmental impact and enhance energy efficiency in transportation.</p></p>
