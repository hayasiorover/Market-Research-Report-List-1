<p><strong>The Digital Ceramic Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Printing</li><li>Dye-sublimation Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital ceramic printing machine market includes two primary types: UV printing and dye-sublimation printing. UV printing uses ultraviolet light to instantly cure or dry ink as it is printed, allowing for vibrant colors and detailed designs on various ceramic surfaces. In contrast, dye-sublimation printing involves transferring dye onto a substrate using heat, resulting in durable and resistant images that penetrate the ceramic material. Both methods offer unique advantages, catering to different needs in custom ceramic decoration and manufacturing.</p></p>
