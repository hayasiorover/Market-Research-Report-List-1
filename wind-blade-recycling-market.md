<p><ul><li>Mechanical Recycling</li><li>Thermal Recycling</li><li>Chemical Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wind blade recycling encompasses three primary types: mechanical, thermal, and chemical recycling. </p><p>Mechanical recycling involves grinding blades into smaller pieces to reuse in composite materials. Thermal recycling employs high-temperature processes to incinerate blades, converting them to energy, while generating heat. Chemical recycling breaks down blades at a molecular level, recovering valuable raw materials for new products. Each method has distinct benefits, addressing the growing demand for sustainable solutions in wind energy, while minimizing landfill waste from decommissioned blades.</p></p>
