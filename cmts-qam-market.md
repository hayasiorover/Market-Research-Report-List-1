<p><p>The CMTS (Cable Modem Termination System) market consists of Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS) types. I-CMTS combines all functions into a single unit, simplifying installation and maintenance, ideal for smaller networks. Conversely, M-CMTS separates functionality into different modules, allowing for greater scalability and flexibility in larger networks, as operators can upgrade specific components independently. Both systems enable efficient broadband connectivity, catering to diverse network requirements and growth strategies in varying market environments.</p></p>
